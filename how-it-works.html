How It Works

A simple workflow that turns raw data into useful insight.

The demo below works as a visual prototype for class. It does not need a full backend yet.

01

Start with company data

Users begin by selecting a company and reviewing key financial data pulled into the platform.

02

Adjust assumptions

Users can enter their own growth rates, margins, or other assumptions instead of relying only on prebuilt estimates.

03

View projection ranges

The platform shows possible outcomes like CAGR, high and low price targets, and comparison views.
